*"She's beautiful,"* she muttered as she looked at the girl on tv walked down the aisle during the pageant. The pregnant woman watching the tv stared in awe as she favored the girl whom she's watching right now. 

*"I wonder what's her name,"* the candidate moved forward and stepped onto the platform to formally introduce herself. She was beautiful and her smile lit up her pretty face as her eyes gleamed a confident glow. 

*"Good evening, I am Allysa Kaye,"* the pregnant woman adored her name as much as she adored her beauty. 

*"Now I know what to name her."*

And on January 21, 2003, a beautiful infant came into the world. 

*"Her name is Allysa Khae."*

And that's how I got my name in this life.
